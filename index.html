− Night storage heaters

The most cost-effective form of electric central heating uses night storage heaters. These heaters use electricity supplied at a cheaper ‘night-time’ rate to heat up special heat-retaining bricks. These bricks then heat your home around the clock using the heat stored in the bricks. Night storage heaters give out heat slowly and are designed to keep warm for the whole of the following day.
